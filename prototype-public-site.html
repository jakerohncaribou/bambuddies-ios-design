We believe the best games happen with friends
BamBuddies started because getting four players together for mahjong shouldn't require six group texts and a spreadsheet. We built the social organizing app we wished existed.

Our mission

Make organizing social games effortless so more people can play together more often. Start with mahjong, expand to all the games you love.

Why mahjong?

Mahjong brings generations together. It's social, strategic, and deeply connected to culture. The community deserves better tools.

Built by Caribou Games

BamBuddies is the first app from Caribou Games, a company building social tools for traditional games. Coming soon to other communities (bridge, poker, mahjong variants worldwide).
